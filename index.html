PubLedge · v0.1 · drafting in public

An open recordkeeping protocol for written interpretations.

PubLedge publishes Joint Interpretation Agreements (JIAs), Regulatory Mitigation Agreements (RMAs), no-action letters, private letter rulings, and analogous fact-specific written interpretations between two parties — in plain markdown, with machine-readable schemas bound to an open upper ontology.

This site is drafting in public. Templates and instances published here are suggested prior art, not official output of any regulator. See each instrument's disclaimer for scope.

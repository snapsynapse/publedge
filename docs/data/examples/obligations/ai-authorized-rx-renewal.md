## Summary

During the mitigation period, a participant may use its AI technology to authorize the renewal of a verified prescription for a Utah resident and issue such prescriptions to a pharmacist licensed under Utah Code §58-17b. Mitigation applies solely to the renewal workflow described in the Proposal; it does not extend to new prescriptions, other services, or products outside the authorized scope.

## What Counts

- Verified existing prescription for a Utah resident
- AI technology authorizes renewal following the processes in the participant's Proposal
- Prescription issued to a pharmacist licensed under §58-17b
- Renewal within the on-formulary medication scope (where applicable under the agreement)

## What Does Not Count

- First-time prescriptions or new therapeutic orders
- Prescriptions to non-Utah residents
- Medications outside the approved formulary
- Services other than renewal offered on the participant's website or platform

## Statute Anchors

- Utah Code §58-17b — Pharmacy licensure
- Utah Code §26B-4-704 — Telehealth requirements
- Utah RMAs 2025-002 (Doctronic) §15(A) and 2026-001 (Legion) §16(A)

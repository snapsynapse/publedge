## Summary

An RMA participant must notify OAIP within 24 hours of any incident that results in harm to the health, safety, or financial well-being of a user of the mitigated technology.

## What Counts

- Harm to user health (physical or mental)
- Harm to user safety
- Harm to user financial well-being
- Notification delivered within 24 hours of the incident occurring
- Notification delivered regardless of whether the harm was caused by the AI or surrounding product behavior

## What Does Not Count

- Internal triage that delays notification beyond the 24-hour window
- Aggregating multiple incidents into a weekly or monthly notification
- Restricting notification to incidents the participant classifies as material

## Statute Anchors

- Agreement-specific incident-notification clause (ElizaChat-style 24-hour rule)

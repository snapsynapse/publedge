## Overview

U.S. Commodity Futures Trading Commission, Division of Swap Dealer and Intermediary Oversight (DSIO). Issues no-action letters and interpretive letters governing registration, compliance, and business-conduct obligations of swap dealers, commodity trading advisors (CTAs), commodity pool operators (CPOs), introducing brokers, and futures commission merchants.

    ## About
    The Utah State Legislature is the bicameral lawmaking body of the State of Utah, comprising the Utah Senate and Utah House of Representatives. Bills enacted by the Legislature and signed by the Governor become codified as Utah Code. PubLedge tracks Utah statutes as first-class records because interpretations issued by Utah OAIP (both Regulatory Mitigation Agreements and Joint Interpretation Agreements) anchor to specific sections of this code.
    For full statutory text and legislative history, see [le.utah.gov](https://le.utah.gov/).

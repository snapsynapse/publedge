## Summary

Before performing any RMA-authorized procedure, a Dentacor-employed hygienist must obtain patient informed consent that explicitly discloses the absence of dentist supervision and explains that a hygienist does not have the full training or scope of practice of a dentist.

## What Counts

- Informed consent obtained from the patient prior to the procedure
- Disclosure that no dentist is supervising the procedure
- Explanation that the hygienist's training and scope of practice is narrower than a dentist's
- Consent obtained and documented for each patient, not a one-time site-wide waiver

## What Does Not Count

- Implied consent or boilerplate intake forms without the absence-of-dentist language
- Consent obtained after the procedure has begun
- Consent that characterizes the hygienist's scope as equivalent to a dentist's

## Statute Anchors

- Utah RMA 2025-001 Section 15(C)(2) — Informed consent requirements

## Summary

ElizaChat must be released in three sequential phases — OAIP trusted-tester cohort, OAIP-reviewed limited student cohort, and full Utah-district student availability — with written OAIP approval required before advancing to each subsequent phase.

## What Counts

- Phase 1: OAIP users only, operating as trusted testers before any student access
- Phase 2: OAIP-reviewed limited group of student users (size and location reviewed by the Office)
- Phase 3: General availability to students across Utah school districts
- Written OAIP approval before each phase transition

## What Does Not Count

- Rolling directly to Phase 2 or 3 without completing Phase 1
- Phase transitions implied by elapsed time rather than OAIP written approval
- Expansions of user population within a phase without OAIP review

## Statute Anchors

- Utah RMA 2024-001 Section VI.H — Phased roll-out
